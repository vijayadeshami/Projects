Importance Of Independence!

Independence, in the context of nations, refers to a state of self-governance and sovereignty, free from external control or interference, especially from colonial powers. It signifies the ability of a nation to make its own decisions and determine its own destiny. The concept of independence is closely tied to the idea of national identity and self-determination
